5. MAINTENANCE AND SUPPORT SERVICES

5.1 Scope. During the period for which Support Fees have been paid, the Licensor shall provide:
(a) Error Corrections for Errors reported by the Licensee;
(b) Updates to the Software as they become generally available;
(c) Telephone and email technical support during Business Hours;
(d) Access to the Licensor's online knowledge base and support portal;
(e) Remote diagnostic assistance.

5.2 Support Hours. "Business Hours" means 6:00 AM to 6:00 PM Pacific Time, Monday through Friday, excluding U.S. federal holidays.

5.3 Error Classification and Response Times. Errors shall be classified and addressed as follows:

(a) Severity 1 — Critical: The Software is inoperable or a core function is unavailable, and no workaround exists. Production operations are halted.
    Response Time: One (1) hour.
    Resolution Target: Eight (8) hours.
    Availability: 24/7, including holidays.

(b) Severity 2 — Major: A major function is significantly impaired, but a workaround is available. Production operations are degraded.
    Response Time: Four (4) hours during Business Hours.
    Resolution Target: Two (2) business days.

(c) Severity 3 — Minor: A minor function is impaired, or a cosmetic defect exists. Production operations are not materially affected.
    Response Time: One (1) business day.
    Resolution Target: Next scheduled Update.

(d) Severity 4 — Enhancement Request: A request for new functionality or improvement.
    Response Time: Five (5) business days (acknowledgment only).
    Resolution: Considered for future releases at the Licensor's discretion.

5.4 Escalation. If the Licensee is not satisfied with the progress on a Severity 1 or Severity 2 Error, it may escalate to the Licensor's Director of Customer Support, and further to the VP of Engineering if unresolved within the Resolution Target.

5.5 Exclusions. Maintenance and Support Services do not cover:
(a) Errors caused by the Licensee's modifications to the Software;
(b) Errors resulting from use of the Software on unsupported hardware or operating systems;
(c) Errors caused by third-party software not approved by the Licensor;
(d) Training or consulting services (available separately);
(e) Data recovery resulting from the Licensee's failure to maintain backups;
(f) Support for versions of the Software that are more than two (2) major versions behind the current release.

5.6 Reinstatement. If the Licensee allows Maintenance and Support to lapse, reinstatement shall require payment of all unpaid Support Fees for the lapsed period plus a reinstatement fee equal to twenty-five percent (25%) of the current annual Support Fee.
